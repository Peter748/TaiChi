Peter Davey is a 7th generation direct lineage holder of the Yang Family Tai Chi tradition, tracing an unbroken line from the great Yang Cheng Fu through Fu Zhong Wen, Fu Sheng Yuan, and Damon Bramich.

Damon Bramich (Fu Wu Xai) is the 6th generation Master and Principal of Newcastle Tai Chi. BaShi close-door disciple of Fu Sheng Yuan for 26 years. World Champion. The first non-family lineage successor after receiving Fu Sheng Yuan's final teachings before his passing in March 2017.

Peter teaches Yang-style Tai Chi and Yang Push Hands (推手) at Golden Lotus Tai Chi, Newcastle — continuing this rare direct transmission from the Yang family heartland.
